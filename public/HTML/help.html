1.1. What is the EV Recharge Bunk App?

The EV Recharge Bunk Application allows you to locate nearby electric vehicle (EV) charging stations, view real-time availability, and easily charge your vehicle using the app. It provides a simple and efficient way to manage your EV charging needs on the go.

1.2. How to Download the App?

You can download the EV Recharge Bunk app from the Google Play Store (for Android) or Apple App Store (for iOS). Simply search for "EV Recharge Bunk" and click on the download button to install the app.

1.3. How to Register for an Account?

Open the app after installation. Click on "Sign Up" or "Create Account." Provide necessary information (name, email, phone number, and vehicle details). Verify your email or phone number through OTP (One Time Password). Once registered, you can log in and start using the app!

Account Management

2.1. How to Log In?

Open the app. Enter your registered email/phone number and password. Tap on "Log In." If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link to reset it.

2.2. How to Update My Profile Information?

Open the app and go to the "Profile" section. Click on "Edit Profile." Modify the necessary details such as your name, vehicle model, or payment information. Save the changes.

2.3. How to Delete My Account?

To delete your account, please reach out to our support team via the Contact Support section, and we will assist you with the account deletion process.

Charging Stations

3.1. How to Find Nearby Charging Stations?

Open the app and navigate to the "Charging Stations" section. Enable location services on your phone. The app will display a list of nearby charging stations with real-time availability, user ratings, and station type (fast or slow charger).

3.2. How to Book a Charging Slot?

Select a nearby charging station from the map or list. Check the available time slots. Choose your preferred time and book the slot. You will receive a booking confirmation along with the estimated charging duration.

3.3. Can I Reserve a Charging Slot in Advance?

Yes, you can reserve a charging slot up to 24 hours in advance. This helps ensure you have access to the charger when you need it most.

Payment & Billing

4.1. How to Add a Payment Method?


Go to the "Payment" section in the app. Click on "Add Payment Method." Enter your payment details (credit/debit card, UPI, or wallet options). Save your payment method for future use.

4.2. How Are Charging Fees Calculated?

Charging fees are based on the type of charger (fast or slow) and the duration of your charge. You can view the pricing details for each station when booking a slot.

4.3. How to View My Billing History?

Go to the "Billing History" section in your profile. Here, you can see the details of all completed transactions, including the time, location, amount charged, and payment method.
