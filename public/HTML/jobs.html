JOB OPPORTUNITIES


1. Project Manager


Responsibilities:

Oversee the entire EV Recharge Application project, ensuring it aligns with goals and timelines. Coordinate between teams (developers, designers, marketing, etc.). Ensure that the project meets deadlines, budgets, and quality standards. Monitor project risks and resolve issues that may arise. Report progress to stakeholders.

2. Software Developer (Backend)

Responsibilities:

Design, develop, and maintain the server-side of the EV recharge application. Integrate APIs for payment gateways, charging station locations, and real-time data. Ensure that the backend is secure, scalable, and optimized. Work closely with frontend developers to ensure a seamless user experience. Troubleshoot and fix any technical issues that arise during development.

3. Software Developer (Frontend)


Responsibilities:

Design and implement the user interface (UI) of the EV Recharge App. Ensure the app is responsive, user-friendly, and provides a smooth experience across devices. Collaborate with backend developers to integrate the necessary features. Optimize the app for high performance and speed. Regularly update the app based on user feedback and testing.

4. Mobile App Developer (iOS/Android)


Responsibilities:

Develop and maintain the mobile version of the EV Recharge application for iOS and Android. Ensure the app is intuitive, stable, and compatible across multiple devices and operating systems. Work with the design team to integrate visual components and features. Optimize mobile apps for performance, battery efficiency, and speed. Implement secure login and payment functionality for mobile users.

5. UI/UX Designer

Responsibilities:

Conduct user research to understand the needs and behavior of EV owners. Design wireframes, prototypes, and final UI designs for the app. Ensure the user experience is intuitive and straightforward. Implement a visually appealing design while keeping usability at the forefront. Collaborate with developers to bring designs to life and maintain consistency across the app.

6. Data Analyst

Responsibilities:

Collect and analyze data related to user behavior, app performance, and charging station usage. Provide insights and recommendations to improve user experience and operational efficiency. Monitor trends and usage patterns to predict future requirements (e.g., location expansion for charging stations). Collaborate with product teams to fine-tune the app's functionality based on data insights.

7. Quality Assurance (QA) Tester

Responsibilities:

Develop and execute manual and automated test cases to ensure the app's quality. Perform functional, performance, security, and usability testing. Identify and report bugs and track them until they are fixed. Work closely with developers to test new features and updates. Ensure the app works flawlessly across devices and platforms.

8. Customer Support Specialist

Responsibilities:

Provide assistance to users with any technical or operational issues they face while using the EV Recharge app. Handle user queries via email, live chat, or phone. Resolve issues related to charging stations, payments, or app functionality. Gather feedback from users and pass it on to the relevant teams for improvements. Offer troubleshooting and guidance on how to use the app effectively.

9. Marketing and Community Outreach Manager

Responsibilities:

Develop and implement strategies to promote the EV Recharge app to potential users. Build partnerships with local businesses and charging station operators. Conduct market research to identify trends in electric vehicle adoption and charging station demand. Manage social media and digital marketing efforts to raise awareness of the app. Coordinate events and campaigns to engage with the EV community.

10. Charging Station Integration Specialist

Responsibilities:

Oversee the integration of EV charging stations into the app. Work with station owners and operators to ensure compatibility with the app. Develop processes for adding and updating charging station data in real-time. Test and troubleshoot any connectivity or operational issues with charging stations. Ensure the accurate display of charging station status, availability, and pricing information within the app.
